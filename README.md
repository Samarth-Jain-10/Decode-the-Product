# Decode-the-Product
This project is structured across three weeks. In the first week, we’ll introduce mentees to the basics of Product Management and key frameworks like MECE, Fishbone Diagrams, 5 WHYs, PESTEL, SWOT, and the 4 Ps, helping them build a clear, structured approach to problem-solving and analysis. The second week will focus on Product Thinking, metrics, and the use of AI tools in PM, where we’ll cover frameworks like CIRCLES, AARRR, and DAU/MAU, along with how AI can assist in research, ideation, and product analysis. In the final week, we’ll work through two Root Cause Analysis (RCA) case studies — one that we’ll solve together, mentors with mentees to demonstrate the process, and another that mentees will take on as a deliverable, applying everything they’ve learned throughout the program. (On the basis of which they’ll also be evaluated)
